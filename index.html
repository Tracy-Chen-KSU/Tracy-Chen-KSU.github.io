ABOUT ME

I joined the College of Aeronautics and Engineering at Kent State University as an assistant professor in August 2019. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Engineering from Case Western Reserve University in 2019 and received my M.S. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Mississippi State University in 2015 under the supervision of Prof. Pan Li. I received my B.E. degree in information engineering from Xidian University, Xi'an, China, in 2012.

My research interests include Cybersecurity (security and privacy in machine learning, Blockchain technology), Artificial intelligence, with applications in the cyber-physical systems (UAV, Robot, power systems) and bioinformatics.
