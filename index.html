ABOUT ME

I joined the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Alabama in Huntsville as an assistant professor in August 2018. I recevied my Ph.D. degree in 2018 from the University of Florida under the supervion of Prof. Yuguang "Michael" Fang and B.Eng. degree from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in 2013.

My research interest is to bring together techniques from convex optimization, probability theory, cryptography and differential privacy to design high-performance networking protocols and secure & privacy-preserving mechanisms for networked systems such as communications networks, mobile health, IoT, CPS, etc.
